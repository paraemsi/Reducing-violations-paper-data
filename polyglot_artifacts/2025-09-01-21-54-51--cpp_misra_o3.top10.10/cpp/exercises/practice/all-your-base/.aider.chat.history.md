#### ## About [Positional Notation][positional-notation]

#### In positional notation, a number in base **b** can be understood as a linear combination of powers of **b**.

####   
#### `(1 × 2⁵) + (0 × 2⁴) + (1 × 2³) + (0 × 2²) + (1 × 2¹) + (0 × 2⁰)`

####   
#### `(1 × 3³) + (1 × 3²) + (2 × 3¹) + (0 × 3⁰)`
